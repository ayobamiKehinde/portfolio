The framework

Force and friction

Every revenue system runs on a flywheel: attract, engage, delight, repeat. Two things act on it: force and friction.

Force spins it faster. Friction cuts against the spin.

The instinct is to add more force or strip out all friction. Both are wrong. Some friction protects quality. Some force burns cash without building anything durable.

Most of the leaks we find aren't missing effort. They're force pointed at the wrong stage, or friction nobody noticed was costing money. The audit finds which is which. The fix keeps the good and removes the bad, and that's where the number gets smaller.

Same flywheel. The audit finds whether you're accelerating it or fighting it.
